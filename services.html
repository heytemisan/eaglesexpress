The chargeable amount will be dependent on the type of service offered. Any change to prior selected optional service must be notified to us before that shipment leaves our USA warehouse to the airport,customers may still be responsible for optional services requested if changes are made when the shipment is already in transit. We will duly refund customers their exact payment if optional service they requested is not rendered.
